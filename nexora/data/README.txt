Contacts file format
-------------------
File: contacts.txt
Format: one contact per line
  Label|Value

Examples:
  Sales & inquiries|hello@nexora.io
  24/7 SOC hotline (clients)|+1 (555) 123-4567
  Office|420 Innovation Drive, Suite 100
  Address line 2|San Francisco, CA 94105
  Business hours|Mon–Fri 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM PT

"Address line 2" is merged with the previous "Office" line on the website.
Lines without a pipe (|) are ignored. Edit contacts.txt and refresh the page to update the Contacts section.
