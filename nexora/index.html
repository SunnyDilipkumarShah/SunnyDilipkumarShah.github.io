About Nexora

Nexora is a cybersecurity company specializing in SOC as a Service—we act as your outsourced Security Operations Center so you get enterprise-grade detection and response without building an in-house SOC.

Our analysts work around the clock from our certified SOC, monitoring your endpoints, networks, and cloud environments. We integrate with your existing SIEM, EDR, and XDR tools—or provide a full managed stack—and deliver clear alerts, playbook-driven response, and regular threat reports.

We serve mid-market and enterprise clients across healthcare, finance, critical infrastructure, and technology. Compliance-ready processes and transparent SLAs are at the core of how we operate.

Products & Services

Full-spectrum SOC as a Service: monitoring, threat intelligence, and response tailored to your environment.

24/7 Threat Monitoring

Continuous surveillance of your endpoints, network, and cloud. Our analysts triage alerts, reduce noise, and escalate confirmed threats with context and recommended actions.

Managed SIEM & XDR

We run your SIEM or XDR platform—ingestion, tuning, use-case development, and correlation rules—so you get actionable visibility without staffing a full SOC team.

Rapid containment and remediation when an incident is confirmed. Playbook-driven response, evidence preservation, and post-incident reports to improve your security posture.

Curated threat intel feeds, IOC integration, and proactive threat hunting to find advanced threats and unknown malware before they cause damage.
